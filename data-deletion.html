Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like your personal data deleted from UCMAS Karapitiya records, please contact us with your request.

How to Request Deletion

You may request deletion by emailing ucmaskarapitiya@gmail.com or calling 0712300900.

Required Details

Please provide your name, phone number, and enough information for us to identify your records correctly.

Processing

We will review and process legitimate deletion requests within a reasonable period, subject to legal or operational record-keeping requirements.
